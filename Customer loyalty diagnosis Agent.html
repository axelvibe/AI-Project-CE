The first-drink cliff

The signup reward is the only near-term reward. After the free drink, the next prize sits about ten visits away. That gap is where the habit was meant to form, and where it dies instead.

The decision rule

Run a 4-week A/B, then scale, pivot, or kill

Saoirse owns the metrics; Cian owns the call. The burden of proof is on survival, not termination.

Scale

Treatment cohort reaches the second reward materially more often and holds higher 90-day retention. Roll out to all 120 stores.

Pivot

If the friction cut alone explains the gain, lead with one-tap earn and simplify the reward ladder.

Kill

Any single tactic with zero measurable signal after 7 days is dropped. No affection-driven survival.
